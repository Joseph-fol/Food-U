Our Services

More than just food, we provide an experience.

Full-Service Event Catering

Whether it’s a wedding for 200 or a gala for 500, we provide comprehensive service including menu planning, professional staffing, table setup, and post-event cleanup.

Corporate & Office Hospitality

Fuel your team and impress your clients with high-quality, reliable catering. We specialize in executive boardroom dining, boxed lunches for trainings, and full buffet spreads for conferences.

Private Chef Experiences

Bring the fine-dining restaurant experience into the comfort of your own home. Our chefs curate bespoke multi-course menus for anniversaries, small gatherings, and VIP guests.

Interactive Food Stations

We offer chef-attended stations such as oyster shucking, carving stations, pasta bars, or flambé desserts. It is not just dinner; it is entertainment that encourages guests to mingle.

Bar & Beverage Services

Elevate your event with our fully managed bar packages. We provide TABC-certified bartenders, signature cocktail creation, sommelier-selected wines, and all necessary mixers, ice, and glassware.

Event Design & Rental Coordination

We coordinate the rental of linens, china, flatware, tables, and chairs to match your aesthetic. We handle the logistics of delivery and setup, so you don't have to juggle multiple vendors.
